# Stakeholder Analysis – CampusFix Residence Maintenance System

## Overview
This document identifies key stakeholders of the CampusFix system and outlines their roles, concerns, pain points, and success metrics.

---

## Stakeholder Table

| Stakeholder | Role | Key Concerns | Pain Points | Success Metrics |
|------------|------|-------------|------------|----------------|
| Students (Residents) | End-users who report maintenance issues | Fast issue reporting, transparency, quick resolution | Delayed repairs, no feedback, manual reporting processes | Issues logged in < 2 minutes, status visibility, resolution within SLA |
| Maintenance Staff | Responsible for resolving reported issues | Clear task assignments, accurate issue details | Poor communication, unclear priorities, duplicated tasks | Tasks assigned instantly, reduced task duplication, completion tracking |
| Residence Manager | Oversees residence operations and maintenance | Monitoring issues, performance tracking, accountability | Lack of reporting tools, poor visibility of unresolved issues | Dashboard visibility, reduced backlog, faster resolution rates |
| System Administrator (IT) | Manages system deployment, maintenance, and uptime | System stability, scalability, security | System crashes, poor documentation, difficult maintenance | 99% uptime, easy deployment, low maintenance overhead |
| University Management | Strategic oversight and decision-making | Reporting, cost efficiency, operational performance | Lack of data insights, inefficient processes | Automated reports, reduced maintenance costs, improved service delivery |
| External Contractors | Third-party service providers for complex issues | Clear job allocation, communication, job tracking | Miscommunication, delays in assignment, unclear scope | Timely job notifications, clear task descriptions, completion confirmation |
| Helpdesk / Support Staff | First line support for system users | Ease of use, quick issue resolution | High volume of repetitive queries, lack of system feedback | Reduced support tickets, faster issue resolution |
