# CampusFix: Student Residence Maintenance and Incident Reporting System

## Project Description
CampusFix is a web-based system designed to help university students report maintenance and infrastructure issues in student residences. The platform enables residence administrators and maintenance staff to manage and resolve reported issues efficiently through a structured workflow.

Currently, many universities rely on informal methods such as verbal communication, WhatsApp messages, or paper logs to report maintenance issues. This leads to delays, poor accountability, and lack of visibility for management.

CampusFix introduces a centralized system where students can submit issues, administrators can assign tasks, and maintenance staff can track and update repair progress.

## Objectives
The objectives of the system are to:

- Digitize the reporting of maintenance issues
- Improve coordination between students and maintenance staff
- Track issue resolution progress
- Improve transparency and accountability
- Provide dashboards for residence administrators
